Veg biryani

Ingrediants

  • Rice: Soak cups aged basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  • Vegetables: Chop 2 cups mixed veggies (potatoes, carrots, French beans, peas, cauliflower).
  • Aromatics: Thinly slice 2 large onions, chop 2 tbsp mint, and 2 tbsp coriander leaves.
  • Marinade: Whisk cup yogurt with 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste, 1 tsp chili powder, tsp turmeric, and 1 tbsp biryani masala.

Veg Biryani is a popular rice dish made by layering fragrant basmati rice with
a spicy,cooked mixture of assorted vegetables, yogurt, and aromatic spices like
saffron and cardamom. Often cooked using the "dum" (slow-steaming) method, this
flavourable, one-pot meal is typically served with raita.

step-by-step instructions

  1. Cook the Rice (80% Done): Boil 5 cups of water with whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cinnamon, star anise, cloves) and 1 tsp salt. Add soaked rice and cook until it is 75-80% done (firm to bite). Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare Vegetable Masala: Heat 2 tbsp ghee in a pot, fry half the sliced onions until golden (birista), then remove for garnish. To the remaining onions, add ginger-garlic paste and sauté. Add the mixed veggies and sauté for 3-4 minutes
  3. Cook Veggies: Add the whisked yogurt marinade, biryani masala, and salt to the vegetables. Cook covered for 5-7 minutes until the veggies are almost cooked but not mushy
  4. Layering: Layer the rice over the veggie gravy. Top with fried onions, coriander leaves, mint leaves, and 1 tbsp saffron milk (soak saffron strands in warm milk)
  5. Dum Cooking: Seal the pot with a tight lid or aluminum foil. Cook on very low heat for 15-20 minutes
  6. Rest and Serve: Let the biryani rest for 10 minutes before gently mixing to avoid breaking the rice grains. Serve hot with raita
